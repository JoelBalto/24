# What It Is
I been wanting to make a game for a while so I decided I would finally start. The goal here is an online multiplayer card game based on the
popular math puzzle 24. The goal of the game is to use 4 cards and the 4 main math operations to make 24 out of the numbers (Jack is 10, Ace is 1, etc.).
The main scope as of now is to make something functional enough to at least be able to play with one other person, mainly my friends. If
it ever becomes possible I can publish it in a way that other people can play it.

# Current Vision of Game
<img width="1706" height="1122" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c0e2d114-adb9-4d7a-9858-89360c615e5d" />

Currently, the vision of the game is to have some sort of poker-like table where 4 cards will spawn on. You can drag and drop cards into one another
and you will see the tentative UI on the bottom for which operation (or the keybinds `Q` `W` `E` `R` above) you would like to perform
on those two cards. Then when you have 24 whether or not it is the sole remaining card you get a glowing effect. For the other players,
you can see how they actively interact with their hand for instance Dinky only has 3 cards indicating he performed some operation on two cards.
Next to Ren you will see some sort of indicator saying that they have solved the set. More to come as the vision comes to fruition.
